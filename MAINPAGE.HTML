WE HELP UKRAINE!

Goldenhelpukraine.org is a platform to help Ukrainian refugees find support worldwide. We aim to reach as many people affected by this war as possible.

HUMANITARIAN AID TO UKRAINE

GoldenHelpUkraine.org makes it a priority to connect people that need help in and out of Ukraine, partnering with citizens and organizations all over europe, that can provide help, regardless of where they are. We are developing this platform in real time and targeting all emerging needs. All contacts will only be used to clarify your responses.

The European Committee of the Regions is in touch with our Ukrainian partners to receive requests for help directly from Ukrainian cities and regional centres. Please send an email to Goldenhelpukraine@gmail.com for further corespondence. Your contribution and collaboration will support our action and provide great help to those in dire need of it.
